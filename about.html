About Me

Zahra Mokhtaran

BIO

My name is Zahra Mokhtaran, and I live in Ottawa. My background is in Urbanism, which I hold a Bachelor of Engineering degree. Also, I have completed the first year of the two-year Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College.

Over the past one year, I’ve worked on several projects in college. My work experience has prepared me to become a landscape photographer which I really enjoy doing it.

I like to call myself a story-teller. A photojournalist who perfectly captures the entire story and puts it all together for you to remember and reminisce on. I’m a hands-on photographer, which means that I’m going to be there to help you every step of the way.

If you are considering me as your photographer or would like to find out more about my work, then please get in touch as I would love to hear your plans!
